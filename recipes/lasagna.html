Lasagna

Description

This recipe describes how to make the best lasagna

Gather the ingredients and follow the instructions and enjoy the best lasagna

Ingredients

  • 300g Ground beef
  • 1 Onions
  • 5 Tomatoes
  • 10 Pasta leafs
  • 250ml Bechamel sauce
  • 200g Cheese

Instructions

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the bechamel sauce.
  4. Layer the lasagna with the top layer as cheese.
  5. Bake for 45 minutes at 350 degrees.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
